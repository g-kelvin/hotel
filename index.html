About Us

Wakalucy Fish Palace

Click to Order via Whatsapp
Whatsapp Chat

Wakalucy Fish Palace is located in Nairobi, Kenya and is a great place to get a delicious cooked fish. Our chefs have been crafting delicious dishes for years and have perfected the art of cooking fish. Our menu offers a variety of fresh fish, including tilapia, trout, and salmon, as well as other seafood options. We also have a variety of side dishes such as Ugali, Mukimo, salads, fries, and rice to accompany your meal. Come and enjoy a delicious meal in our cozy and inviting atmosphere!

  • Fish is known for its delicious, mild flavor that can be enjoyed in a variety of dishes.
  • Fish is an excellent source of protein, vitamins, minerals, and omega-3 fatty acids.

Why Choose Wakalucy?

At our fish restaurant, we provide top-quality, delicious meals that are cooked to perfection. Our menu features a wide variety of seafood, from classic favorites to unique dishes. Our experienced chefs take pride in using the freshest ingredients to create unique and flavorful meals. We strive to make your experience at our restaurant both memorable and enjoyable, and we look forward to serving you soon.
